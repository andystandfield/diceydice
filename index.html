Roll

Instructions

Just enter a standard die formula. Seperate rolls with a comma (e.g. 3d6+2, 1d8+1). If you want multiple rolls of the same kind, use 'x' (e.g. 3d6x6). You can add title to a roll by typing it in square brackets (e.g. [Bob's attack roll]), and add notes between quotes (e.g. "Long sword +1").

Example:

[Bob's damge roll] 2d6+1 "Greghor's almighty hammer of justice!", [Aurora's damage] 1d8+2, 3d6
